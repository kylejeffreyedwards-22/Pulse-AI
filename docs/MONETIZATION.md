# Pulse AI: Vacation Rental Monetization Strategy

How we turn AI-powered property optimization into a high-margin revenue stream.

## 1. AI Listing Audits (The Hook)
We offer property owners a deep-dive analysis of their current VRBO/Airbnb performance.
*   **The Service:** AI scan of titles, descriptions, photo quality, and ranking keywords.
*   **Pricing:** **$99 per audit.**
*   **Goal:** Convert 10% of audits into full management retainers.

## 2. Dynamic Revenue Management (Monthly Retainer)
We take over the daily pricing and listing management for the owner.
*   **The Service:** AI-driven hourly price adjustments, automated guest communication, and search algorithm optimization.
*   **Pricing:** **$199/month per property** OR **5% of total booking revenue** (whichever is higher).
*   **Target:** 20-50 high-value properties in major vacation hubs.

## 3. Real Estate Lead Generation (B2B)
Selling high-signal data to real estate investment firms looking for the next "Hot" rental market.
*   **The Service:** Selling our internal "Investment Scout" data logs.
*   **Pricing:** Custom enterprise quotes ($500+ /month).

## 4. Affiliate Partnerships (Passive)
Recommending high-end furniture, smart locks (August/Yale), and insurance (Hiscox) to owners.
*   **Payout:** 5-15% commission on every referral.
