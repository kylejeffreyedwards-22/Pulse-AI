# Pulse AI: Advanced Monetization Blueprint

How we turn free subscribers into a $10k+/month revenue stream.

## 1. The Ad Network (Immediate Revenue)
Once we hit 1,000 subscribers, we apply for the **Beehiiv Ad Network**. 
* **How it works:** Beehiiv places relevant AI ads in your newsletter automatically.
* **Your job:** Zero.
* **Payout:** $1.50 - $3.00 per unique click.

## 2. Premium Pulse+ Tier ($15/month)
We offer a paid subscription for high-level professionals.
* **What they get:**
    * **The Weekly Deep Dive:** A 10-page analysis of one specific AI sector (e.g., AI in Healthcare).
    * **Agent Prompts:** Copy-paste prompts we use to run the business.
    * **Early Access:** Research agents find news 12 hours before they hit the main feed.

## 3. Affiliate Launches (The Big Wins)
When we feature a new AI tool (like a new CRM or Image Gen), we use an affiliate link.
* **The Deal:** If a reader signs up, you get 20-40% of their subscription fee every month.
* **Goal:** Feature 1 high-quality tool per week.

## 4. Sponsored Deep Dives ($500 - $2,000 per post)
AI startups are desperate for eyeballs.
* **The Deal:** A startup pays you to write a dedicated 'how-to' guide using their tool.
* **Agent Flow:** I research the startup, draft the guide, and you just hit 'Publish'.

## 5. Enterprise Data Feeds
Companies want to know what AI tech their competitors are using.
* **The Deal:** We sell a raw data feed of our 'Research Agent' logs to corporate strategy teams.
